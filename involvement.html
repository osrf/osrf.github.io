Get Involved

The best way to get involved with OSRF is to work with some of our sponsored projects such as ROS and Gazebo. These projects have large established communities and user bases. Before contributing code to one of our sponsored projects, please make sure that we have a signed Contributor Agreement on file.

If you’d like to meet like-minded roboticists, check out our supported and affliated events.

If you would like some OSRF (or ROS or Gazebo) logo gear, check out the OSRF online store.
